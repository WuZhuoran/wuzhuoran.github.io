  • Completeness:
    • ‘‘A is preferred to B,’’
    • ‘‘B is preferred to A,’’
    • ‘‘A and B are equally attractive.’’
  • Transitivity:
    • ‘‘A is preferred to B, B is preferred to C, so A is preferred to C’’
  • Continuity:
    • If ‘‘A is preferred to B’’, then situations suitably ‘‘close to’’ A must also be preferred to B
